Due:

  • Bring your Fabric Exploration pin-proofs for review.

Before Class:

  • Bring the following materials to class:
    • Laptop or Tablet
    • Medium weight muslin
    • Alphanumeric Pattern Paper
    • Needlepoint tracing wheel
    • Ticonderonga #2 Pencil
    • Pencil sharpener
    • Tape measure
    • Ball head quilter's pins
    • 1" small silk pins
    • Fabric Scissors
    • Fine-tip markers in various colors
    • 14” x 24” large L-Square ruler
    • Fashion French curve/Armsyce ruler
    • Fashion Hip Curve ruler

During Class:

  • Fabric Exploration Discussion
  • Using different shapes to create unusual silhouettes.
  • Students will use 3 different geometric shapes (circles, triangles, squares, rectangles, etc.) to explore draping principles and how they can bend/break the rules to create interesting silhouettes by experimenting with these shapes.
    • Students will drape, mark, true, and pin-proof only.
    • The four pin-proofed garments are to be photographed and submitted in a single PDF presentation with the original photo, technical flat, and written analysis of the work.

After Class:

  • Complete and submit Fabric Exploration Assignment via Blackboard > Submissions by the end of day.
    • All drapes should be photographed on the form, trued, and pin-proofed. These images will be compiled with photos of the patterns, construction process, final garment photos, and final technical flats to create one multi-page PDF file and submitted to Blackboard > Submissions.
  • Continue working on the 4 drapes for the Draping with Shapes assignment.
    • Take photos to document your process and outcome.
    • Create flat drawings for your designs.
