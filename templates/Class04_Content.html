Before Class:

  • Bring the following materials to class:
    • Laptop or Tablet
    • Medium weight muslin
    • Alphanumeric Pattern Paper
    • Needlepoint tracing wheel
    • Ticonderonga #2 Pencil
    • Pencil sharpener
    • Tape measure
    • Ball head quilter's pins
    • 1" small silk pins
    • Fabric Scissors
    • Fine-tip markers in various colors
    • 14” x 24” large L-Square ruler
    • Fashion French curve/Armsyce ruler
    • Fashion Hip Curve ruler

During Class:

  • Make any neccessary changes to the dress drape.

After Class:

  • True all pieces and draft the desired sleeve.
  • Transfer all drapes to pattern paper.
  • Add all neccessary notation to the garment patterns.
  • Be sure to include any additional drafts on pattern paper (i.e. facings, linings, interlinings, etc.)
  • Complete dress technical flats, dress pin proof, finalize all transferred and notated patterns and bring all work to our next class meeting.
